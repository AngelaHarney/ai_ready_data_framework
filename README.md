# ai_ready_data_framework
Open standards AI Ready Data Framework

Framework Outline

Step 1 — Creating an Intelligence Layer of Data Architecture
Step 2 — Define Clear AI Objectives
Step 3 — Conduct a Data Audit
Step 4 — Establish Common Business Definitions
Step 5 — Cleanse and Standardize
Step 6 — Enrich and Integrate
Step 7 — Structure and Label for Learning
Step 8 — Ensure Security and Compliance
Step 9 — Automate Curated Data with Pipelines
Step 10 — Store Development, Training, Validation, Testing and Output Datasets in Intelligence Layer
Step 11 — Validate, Monitor and Certify Continuously
